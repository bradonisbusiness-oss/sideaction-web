# SideAction Instagram Carousel 01 — Betting Disputes

## Goal
Turn a common pain point into waitlist clicks.

## Audience
People already making sports bets, challenge bets, money bets, or punishments with friends.

## Format
- 6 slides
- Dark background
- Use real app screenshots where possible
- CTA points to `sideactionapp.com`

## Caption
You do not need a bigger betting group.
You need a cleaner way to lock in what was actually said.

Most friend-group bet drama is not about the game.
It is about:
- terms changing later
- proof getting buried
- unpaid losses turning into arguments

That is why we built SideAction.

If your group already makes bets, get on the private beta waitlist.

sideactionapp.com

## Slide script

### Slide 1 — Hook
**Headline:**
The bet was fun.
The argument after was not.

**Subtext:**
Most side bets break down after the game, not before it.

**Visual:**
Big headline only. Keep it clean.

---

### Slide 2 — Problem
**Headline:**
Group chats are a terrible place to track bets.

**Bullets:**
- Original terms get buried
- Screenshots go missing
- Nobody remembers the same payout

**Visual:**
Use `assets/feed-shot.png` blurred or darkened in the background.

---

### Slide 3 — What SideAction fixes
**Headline:**
Lock the terms before it starts.

**Bullets:**
- who is in
- what is on the line
- what counts as proof
- whether it is paid or still unpaid

**Visual:**
Use `assets/create-shot.png`.

---

### Slide 4 — Why it matters
**Headline:**
No more "that is not what we said."

**Subtext:**
Everyone sees the same record from start to payout.

**Visual:**
Use a simple callout over `assets/profile-shot.png`.

---

### Slide 5 — Social pressure angle
**Headline:**
Unpaid losses should stay visible.

**Subtext:**
SideAction makes it obvious who pays up and who ducks it.

**Visual:**
Use a status badge treatment: Won / Lost / Paid / Unpaid.

---

### Slide 6 — CTA
**Headline:**
If your group already makes bets, join the private beta.

**Bullets:**
- no app install yet
- no payment info needed
- first invite wave goes to the waitlist

**CTA:**
Get on the list at sideactionapp.com

## Story version
Use slides 1, 3, and 6 as IG stories with a link sticker to the waitlist.

## Why this angle
This is the clearest pain on the landing page and the easiest one for people to instantly recognize in their own group chat.
