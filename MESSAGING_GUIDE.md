# SideAction Messaging Guide

## Core problem
People make bets in group chats, DMs, and text threads.
Later, someone loses and rewrites the bet.
That is the pain.

## Core promise
SideAction puts the bet on record before it starts.
One bet. One receipt. One version of the truth.

## Positioning
- Not a sportsbook
- Not a casino
- Not a wallet
- Not a place that holds money
- It is a receipt and tracking layer for bets between friends

## Messaging rules
1. Lead with the argument people already hate.
2. Use plain words like bet, stake, proof, loser, pay up, receipt.
3. Keep one main CTA: join beta waitlist.
4. Show trust fast: no payment info, no money custody, no spam.
5. Make the customer the hero: they stop the argument by locking the deal early.
6. Negative framing beats clever framing.

## Hero options
- Stop the "that's not what we bet" fight.
- Lock the terms before the game starts.
- Keep one receipt after it ends.

## Best-fit audience
People who already bet with friends on:
- sports games
- prop bets
- punishments
- side deals
- challenge bets

## Proof points to repeat
- both sides see the same terms
- stake is locked before the event
- proof stays attached to the bet
- SideAction does not hold money
- reputation follows who settles clean

## CTA copy bank
- Join Beta Waitlist
- Get Beta Access
- Get first access before the next bet turns into a fight

## Words to avoid
- revolutionize
- gamify
- seamless
- ecosystem
- next-gen
- community-first platform

## Quick test for any new copy
If a first-time visitor cannot answer these in 5 seconds, the copy failed:
1. What problem does this solve?
2. Is this for me?
3. What should I do next?
