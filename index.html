No. SideAction does not set odds, take bets, or hold money. It is the receipt layer for bets people already make with friends.

No. SideAction is for locking terms, tracking outcomes, and keeping receipts. It does not ask for payment info on the waitlist.

Sports bets, prop bets, challenge bets, punishments, and side deals between friends. If people already make the bet in texts or DMs, it fits.
