Before you join

Is SideAction a sportsbook or gambling app?

No. SideAction does not set odds, take bets, or hold money. It is the receipt layer for bets people already make with friends.

Does SideAction hold the money?

No. SideAction is for locking terms, tracking outcomes, and keeping receipts. It does not ask for payment info on the waitlist.

How do we actually pay each other?

However your group already does it. Venmo, cash, e-transfer, Cash App, or settle up in person. SideAction locks the receipt and tracks who owes what. It does not sit in the middle of the payment.

What kind of bets is this for?

Sports bets, prop bets, challenge bets, punishments, and side deals between friends. If people already make the bet in texts or DMs, it fits.

Why join now if beta is private?

Waitlist members get first shot at invite waves, product updates, and a say in what gets built before public launch.

Do I need my whole betting group on SideAction first?

No. Join yourself first. Solo signup is fine. Once you get access, you can bring in the same friends you already bet with.
