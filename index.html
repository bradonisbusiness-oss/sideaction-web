How it works

Make the bet clear before anyone can argue about it later

Most bets do not fail because of the game. They fail because the terms were loose, the proof was fuzzy, or nobody wants to chase payout after the fact. SideAction fixes that in three simple steps.

01

Create the receipt

Write the bet once so both sides can see the same terms before anything starts.

  • Who is in the bet
  • What the stake is
  • What counts as a win or loss
02

Attach the proof rule

Decide up front what proof settles it so nobody can move the goalposts later.

  • Screenshots, video, or receipts
  • Deadline and outcome rules
  • Shared record both sides can point to
03

Track payup cleanly

When the bet ends, SideAction keeps settlement visible without turning it into awkward personal chasing.

  • See who paid and who still owes
  • Send neutral reminders
  • Keep future bets cleaner with a real record
