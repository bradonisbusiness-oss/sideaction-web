How SideAction works

  • Make the stake clear from the start

Track what happened after the bet.

  • See the result clearly

Common objections

The fast answers before you join.

Is this only for sports bets?

No. Sports is the wedge. SideAction also fits gym bets, content challenges, business dares, punishments, and any side deal where people need the terms and proof in one place.

Do we need to bet real money?

No. Use cash, chores, punishments, bragging rights, or anything else your group cares about. The point is making the terms clear and the follow-through visible.

Why not just use the group chat?

Because chats bury the original terms, screenshots get lost, and nobody agrees later. SideAction keeps the bet, the proof, and the payout status on record from start to finish.
