# React Signs - Business Notes

**Website:** reactsigns.com
**Tagline:** Your Local Advertising Experts
**Positioning:** Physical + Digital advertising for local businesses

## 4 Core Services

1. **Rent a Sign** — Curbside/mobile billboard rentals, high daily impressions
2. **Smart Display** — Digital screens & menu boards for in-store engagement, remote control
3. **Local Digital Ads** — Shared screen network; host a screen → get free ads at other host locations across the network (most scalable product)
4. **Commercial Interruption** — Sports bar & pub ad software; interrupts events to play in-house promos and local ads

## Business Model
- Local advertising network
- Partner model (Become a Partner + Partner Login = likely local operators/franchisees running the model in new markets)
- Multiple locations (expansion underway)
- Full-stack: physical signage + digital screens + software

## Key Observations
- Distribution-first business — selling local impressions/attention
- Digital Ad Network is the compounding, scalable play
- Partner program suggests franchise/operator expansion model
- Bradon is the face of the brand (appears in marketing photo with Subway sign)
